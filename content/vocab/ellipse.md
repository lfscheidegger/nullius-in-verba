> **Draft placeholder.** Vocabulary stub for exercising the generator.

An ellipse is the set of points whose distances to two fixed points — the
*foci* — sum to a constant. Pin a loop of string at the foci, pull it taut
with a pencil, and trace: that curve is an ellipse. In polar coordinates
centered on one focus,

$$
r = \frac{p}{1 + e\cos\theta},
$$

where $e$ is the *eccentricity*: $e = 0$ is a circle, and the curve
stretches as $e \to 1$.
